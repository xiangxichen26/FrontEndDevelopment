Cavapoo

The Cavapoo, a charming crossbreed between the Cavalier King Charles Spaniel and the Poodle, is celebrated for its friendly, affectionate nature and hypoallergenic coat. This delightful mix combines the Cavalier's gentle, loving demeanor with the intelligence and low-shedding qualities of the Poodle, making Cavapoos ideal companions for a wide range of families, including those with allergy sufferers. They typically inherit a soft, curly or wavy coat that can come in a variety of colors, including cream, gold, red, and sometimes even patterns like those of their Cavalier parents.
